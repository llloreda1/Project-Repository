Chelsea's Stadium Stamford Bridge

Stamford Bridge is a football stadium in Fulham, adjacent to the borough of Chelsea in South West London. It is the home of Premier League club Chelsea. With a capacity of 40,834, it is the ninth largest venue of the 2021 and 2022 Premier League season."Stamford Bridge" is considered to be a derivative of "Samfordesbrigge" meaning "the bridge at the sandy ford". Eighteenth century maps show a "Stanford Creek" running along the route of what is now a railway line at the back of the East Stand as a tributary of the Thames.
